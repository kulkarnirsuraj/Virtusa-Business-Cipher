About

When it comes to appeals and grievances, healthcare organizations are struggling to increase productivity, improve quality scores, minimize penalties, enhance cash flow visibility and provider satisfaction.

With unpredictable volume, varying service agreement levels and concerns ranging from simple procedural issues to matters of life and death, managing insurance appeals and grievances (A&G) is a complex, labor-intensive process, especially when managed manually.

ForeTell helps healthcare payers to Process high volumes quickly and more accurately, Improves response time for providers and members (user) which improves provider satisfaction and productivity of the organization. Our idea of automating much of the A&G process and reporting, on basis of CPT and ICD codes, which helps to lower the cost and risk, greater efficiency and productivity, and improved payer satisfaction and additional reporting allows management greater process insight.
